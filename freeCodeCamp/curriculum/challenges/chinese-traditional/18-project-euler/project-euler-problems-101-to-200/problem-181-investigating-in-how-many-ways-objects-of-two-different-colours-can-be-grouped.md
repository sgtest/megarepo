# --description--

$$(BBBW)\\;(B,BBW)\\;(B,B,BW)\\;(B,B,B,W)\\;(B,BB,W)\\;(BBB,W)\\;(BB,BW)$$

In how many ways can sixty black objects $B$ and forty white objects $W$ be thus grouped?
