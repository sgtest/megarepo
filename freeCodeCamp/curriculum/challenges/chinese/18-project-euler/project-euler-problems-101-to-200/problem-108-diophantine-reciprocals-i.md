# --description--

In the following equation x, y, and n are positive integers.

$$\frac{1}{x} + \frac{1}{y} = \frac{1}{n}$$

对于 `n` = 4，正好有三种不同的解决方案：

$$\begin{align}   & \frac{1}{5} + \frac{1}{20} = \frac{1}{4}\\\\
  \\\\   & \frac{1}{6} + \frac{1}{12} = \frac{1}{4}\\\\
  \\\\ & \frac{1}{8} + \frac{1}{8} = \frac{1}{4} \end{align}$$

`n` 中不同解数超过一千的最小值是多少？
